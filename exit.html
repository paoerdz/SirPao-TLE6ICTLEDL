Beyond the Chalk and Forms:

Finding Your Joy in DepEd

Life as a public school teacher in the Philippines is a unique blend of passion, sacrifice, and an endless stream of paperwork. It's easy to get lost in the stress of compliance and deadlines. But within the walls of our classrooms and the fabric of our school communities, there are countless moments of light, humor, and profound satisfaction.

One way of attracting good things that you want to happen is to share your story to anyone who wants to hear it.

Describe to them a specific moment or a personal philosophy that you have developed to consciously choose joy and reduce stress in your teaching career.

Did a student's simple "thank you" reframe your entire week? Did you discover a brilliant way to streamline your ONE-KIND-OF-A-HELL tasks? Have you and your colleagues created a support system to laugh through the challenges?

Tell them how you actively make your life in DepEd not just survivable, but genuinely fulfilling. Let's create a collection of stories that reminds us why we started this journey in the first place!
